Capture lost crossing onto the scrollbar

Top-level page, no iframe. The page overflows so a vertical scrollbar appears. Capture is dropped when the captured pointer leaves the page's viewport and crosses into browser chrome — the scrollbar here, though the toolbar and a docked DevTools pane do it too.

  1. Press the box (turns green) and hold the button.
  2. Drag the cursor right onto the scrollbar and release there.
  3. Repeat several times — intermittent, roughly 1 in 3.

Pass (no bug): the box turns gray — its own pointerup ran. Fail (bug): the box stays green — lostpointercapture fired on the box before any release, the pointerup retargeted to BODY/HTML above the box, and the box's own listener never saw it. Only the document listener did.
